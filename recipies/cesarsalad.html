Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 large romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 lemon (for squeezing)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Grilled chicken for added protein
  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce, then place it in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle olive oil over the lettuce and sprinkle with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine.
  3. Add croutons and grated Parmesan cheese to the salad.
  4. Pour Caesar dressing over the mixture and toss well to ensure everything is coated evenly.
  5. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the salad for a zesty kick.
  6. Optionally, add grilled chicken slices on top for extra protein.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
